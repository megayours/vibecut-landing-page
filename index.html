How it works

01

A branding kit, a character, a concept, a reference, a mood, a rough direction.

02

03

04

Vibecut learns from what you choose, what you change, and what you keep.

05

Vibecut autonomously generates new brand-aligned video every day.

06

Vibecut detects trending formats, emerging aesthetics, and viral creative patterns, then turns those signals into new content aligned with your style.
